Rules

As a detective or a traitor, you can access the special equipments shop by pressing your "Show Context Menu" key (C by default).

Global chat is accessed by pressing Y and team chat is accessed by pressing U.

You can talk on the global voice chat by pressing X and on the team voice chat (when you're a traitor) by pressing SHIFT

Propsurfing is allowed on this server, but can lead to deadly falls.

Your karma is reset every hour, it goes down by damaging teammates, and up by damaging enemies

To access the damagelog, press F8 after the round has finished."

If you are stuck in spectator mode, press F1, go to the settings tab and uncheck the "spectator-only" mode

You can report players by typing "!report" in chat or by clicking on the popup widow when you die.

You can mute a specific player by holding TAB and clicking the sound icon next to their name..
